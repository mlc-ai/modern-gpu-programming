Scale Factors in TMEM (nvfp4)

warpx4: one logical scale → 4 lanes  ·  R[4 : 32@TLane]

Logical SFA  (M × SF_K)

M = 128, SF_K = 4  ·  cell = SFA[m, sfk]

TMEM  (128 lanes × words)

lane = m%32 (+32/+64/+96) · col = m//32 · byte = sfk
Hover a scale factor on the left
Each logical SFA[m, sfk] is stored once (TLane = m%32, word column = m//32, byte = sfk) and broadcast warpx4 to lanes { m%32, +32, +64, +96 }.
